Richard Owen Holmes Montgomery

Richard Owen Holmes Montegomery, better known as Ricky Montogomery, is an American singer-songwriter from Los Angeles, California. He is born in April 3, 1993. Since he was 14 years old, he was already interested in music and wanted a career that involved public attention. With that as his goal, he started a Vine account during the peak of 2014, which gained him quite an audience. He then released his first EP (Extended play; a music recording) and dropped out of college. In 2016, he found a studio and finally started to record his first songs. He started a band called "The Honeysticks", but later disbanded due to personal reasons.

He took a year break from music for a while because he was not earning any money from it. He considered quitting until he launched his supposed last ever song titled "Line without a Hook" during the peak of the COVID-19 pandemic. This song blew up on tiktok, causing him instant fame. His song was used for anime edits and sad background scenarios. He then launched another song named 'Mr. Loverman', which also blew up in social media. From there on, he signed a contract with Warner studios. His most recent popular song is "Boy Toy"
